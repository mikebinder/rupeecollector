Legend of Zelda Rupee Collector

Game Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four rupees below. By clicking on a rupee you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game if your total score equals the random number. You will lose if your score exceeds the random number.

The value of each rupee is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, both the target score and the value of the rupees will be randomly generated.
